# LinkedIn Post 适配模板

## 格式要求
- **字数甜区：** 800-1500 字符（~150-300 词）效果最好
- **最大字数：** 3000 字符
- **语言：** 英文
- **换行：** 多用换行，每段 1-2 句，避免大段文字
- **Hook：** 前两行决定是否展开，必须抓人

## 结构

```
[Hook — 1-2 句，制造好奇或共鸣]

[空行]

[正文 — 分段短小，每段一个点]

[空行]

[结尾 — CTA 或 takeaway]

[空行]

[3-5 个 hashtag]
```

## 平台特色元素
- ✅ 用 emoji 作为 bullet point（适度，2-5 个）
- ✅ 用数字列表（"3 things I learned..."）
- ✅ 问题结尾（引发评论）
- ✅ 个人经历 > 通用建议
- ❌ 不放外链在正文（降权），链接放评论区
- ❌ 不用超过 5 个 hashtag
- ❌ 不在发布后 10 分钟内编辑

## Hashtag 策略
- 3-5 个，混合大小：1 个大标签 + 2-3 个中标签 + 1 个垂直标签
- 示例：#AI #AIAgents #SoftwareEngineering #FounderLife #OpenSource

## 发布时间（悉尼时区）
- 最佳：周二-周四 早 7-8 点 AEST（= 北美前一天下午）
- 次佳：周一、周五 早 7-8 点 AEST

## 示例

```
I spent 3 months building an AI agent system that runs 24/7 on my Mac Studio.

Here's what actually worked (and what didn't):

🔧 What worked:
• Multi-agent coordination through Discord channels
• Skill-based architecture (each capability = one markdown file)
• Watchdog crons that auto-escalate stalled tasks

❌ What didn't:
• Letting agents run unsupervised for hours
• Over-engineering the memory system
• Trusting sub-agents to report back on their own

The biggest lesson?

AI agents are like junior devs — they need clear specs, deadlines, and someone checking their work.

What's your experience with AI agents? 👇

#AI #AIAgents #SoftwareEngineering #FounderLife
```
